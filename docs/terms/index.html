Description of Service

SendDB provides various features including but not limited to:

  • Tracking levels that are sent
  • Providing leaderboards for creators and levels
  • Notifying users when followed creators' levels are sent
  • Allowing users to suggest ratings and difficulties for levels
  • Offering trending level information

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you use SendDB, we collect and process the following information:

  • User IDs: We store Discord user IDs to track follows, suggestions, and moderator status
  • Follow Preferences: We store information about which creators and levels you choose to follow
  • Commands Usage: We track command usage for statistical purposes
  • Level Suggestions: We store your suggestions for level difficulties and ratings

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing notifications when followed creators or levels are sent
  • Generating leaderboards and trending level information
  • Computing suggestion accuracy and weighting user suggestions
  • Improving the Bot's functionality based on usage patterns
  • Maintaining moderation systems for level suggestions

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your data may be publicly visible in the following circumstances:

  • When your suggestion contributes to a level's average rating (without identifying you personally)
  • If you are a moderator and your name appears in moderation-related activities

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a MongoDB database with appropriate security measures in place. While we implement reasonable security practices, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Moderator Access

Bot moderators have access to suggestion data to review and rate levels, but they cannot access your personal follow preferences or other private information.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View what information we store about you using the /my-suggestions and /follow list commands
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information by updating your suggestions
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data by contacting us (see Contact Information)
  • Restriction: Limit what data we collect by unfollowing creators/levels and discontinuing use of suggestion features

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you request deletion of your data, we will remove it from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
